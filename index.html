In 2007, the first ever branch of TNC (known before as TheNet.Com) was established. It was an experimental attempt from the Founder as he had extra computer units that its original buyer did not collect. A misfortune turned into an opportunity. From then on, the computer shop business prospered and entered into franchising in 2012.

By 2009, TNC was able to establish  more than 190 branches nationwide. Despite the challenging times, as of February 2024, TNC is still the leading cyber cafe franchise chain in the Philippines.

TNC was able to expand its business ventures into different sectors of gaming and esports. Now, TNC owns a franchise slot in the biggest Mobile Legends league in the Philippines.

Also, TNC has ventured into events management, production, broadcast, and full marketing services specializing in gaming and esports events.

TNC continues to innovate and look for opportunities in the esports and gaming landscape.
